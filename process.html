Our Process

From Harvest to Export: Our Commitment to Excellence.

1

Harvesting

Skilled collectors carefully tap the trees in the wild woodlands of the Horn of Africa. We use sustainable methods to ensure tree longevity and forest health.

2

Transportation

Raw resin is collected from remote areas and transported to our central sorting centers using 4x4 vehicles specially equipped for rugged terrain.

3

Sorting & Grading

At our facilities, the gum is manually cleaned, sorted, and graded by size, color, and purity to meet specific international standards (e.g., Pea size, Grade 1).

4

Storage

Processed resins are stored in climate-controlled warehouses to maintain freshness and prevent clumping or degradation before shipment.

5

Exporting

We handle all logistics, documentation, and customs clearance to ship your order via sea or air freight to any port in the world.
