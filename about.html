Our Heritage & Journey

Eagum Exporters Limited began as a vision to connect the legendary aromatic treasures of the Horn of Africa with discerning buyers worldwide. For over 15 years, we've built a reputation for delivering uncompromising quality and ethical practices.

Based in Nairobi, Kenya, we operate across the primary harvesting regions of Kenya, Somalia, Ethiopia, and Tanzania—where ancient traditions of resin tapping have been perfected over centuries. Our commitment is simple: preserve the integrity of these natural products while supporting the communities who harvest them.

Company History & Establishment

Eagum Exporters Limited was established in 2009 in Nairobi, Kenya. Founded by specialists in botanical resins and trade logistics, the company grew from a small trader connecting harvesters to local buyers into a full-service exporter with international certifications and a global client base.

Our name reflects our focus: 'Eagum' — East African Gums — highlighting our roots and dedication to the region's natural products. Over the years we've formalised processing, quality systems, and direct purchasing agreements with harvesting cooperatives to guarantee traceability and consistent supply.

How We Work & Our Process

  1. Sourcing: Direct procurement from vetted harvesters and cooperatives in Kenya, Somalia, Ethiopia and Tanzania.
  2. Initial Grading: Field sorting to remove contaminants and pre-grade resin types at collection points.
  3. Processing & Drying: Controlled drying and cleaning to ensure stable moisture and purity.
  4. Laboratory Testing: COAs and lab analysis for pesticide residues, moisture, and purity when required.
  5. Packing & Documentation: Customer-specific packing, clear batch labeling, and full export paperwork including phytosanitary and COA where applicable.
  6. Logistics & Shipping: Coordinated sea/air freight with trusted partners, including insurance and customs support.

Markets We Target

We serve commercial buyers across Europe, Asia, the Americas, and Africa—specialising in suppliers for fragrance houses, pharmaceutical manufacturers, food and beverage formulators, and wholesale distributors who require consistent, certified commodities.

How We Work With Buyers
  • Transparent Pricing: Clear, competitive quotes with packaging and logistics options.

  • Custom Packaging: Options for bulk bags, drums, or retail-ready packs to suit buyer needs.
  • Dedicated Support: Direct account management, fast responses to technical queries, and reliable shipment tracking.
